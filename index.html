About TGC27

Strength for the work that lasts.

Whether in the first century or the twenty‑first, faithful endurance has always been the cost of discipleship. But you weren't meant to endure in your own strength.

At TGC27, we'll open God's Word together and discover The Power to Persevere.

Through the lens of Revelation's seven letters, we'll learn to stay centered on the gospel, focused on our mission, and faithful to our calling.
